ABOUT RECREATIONAL CLOTHING

Recreational Clothing is a brand that specializes in sustainable, minimalist streetwear. Their clothing appeals to Gen-Z's values of sustainability and inclusivity while offering trendy affordable pieces. The brand launched in 2021 and is looking to scale up its presence online.

RESEARCH ANALYSIS

Gen-Z Characteristics

  • Values authenticity, social justice and sustainability
  • Prefers short form visually engaging content
  • Engages with brands that reflect their identity and support causes they care about

Target Demographic

  • Age 16-25 years old
  • Located in urban areas
  • Interested in streetwear, thrifting, sustainability, pop culture and emerging trends
  • Are on Instagram and TikTok
